Institution of Eminence Status for Amrita

Govt. of India has awarded Institution of Eminence tag to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham to emerge as World Class Teaching and Research Institution. Our goal is to be in top 500 of world ranking in the next 10 years and in top 100 eventually. The aim of the scheme is to bring higher educational institutions selected as IoEs in top 500 of world ranking in the next 10 years and in top 100 eventually overtime.

The World University Rankings 2020

In the 2020 edition of the annual rankings released, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has been placed in the list of 601-800 category. The ranking features universities in 70 countries and seventeen Indian higher education institutions have featured in the list of the world's best universities.
